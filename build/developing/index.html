How does an Auto Warranty Work?

Get a Customized Coverage Plan – Select a plan that best fits your needs. Start by getting a free, no-obligation quote.

Choose Your Repair Facility – Extended Auto Warranties can be used at licensed repair facilities throughout the United States. These repair facilities include independent facilities, nationwide chains (Example: Goodyear, Pep Boys, Firestone, etc.) or even dealers.

Repair Facility Submits Claim on Your Behalf – Once the facility has diagnosed the vehicle before making a claim, they will submit the information to the vehicle service contract’s claims department for approval.

Claims department pays the repair department directly – When your repair is complete, the payment will be made by the vehicle service contract directly to the repair facility.

Our Company uses the terms ‘Warranty’ and ‘Extended Warranty’ with ‘Vehicle Service Contract’ and ‘Extended Service Contract’ for marketing purposes only. * A Vehicle Service Contract (VSC) is often referred to as an "auto warranty" or an "extended car warranty," but it is not a warranty. A VSC does, however, provide repair coverage for your vehicle after the manufacturer’s car warranty expires. A VSC is a contract between you and a VSC provider or administrator that states what a covered repair is and what is not.
